## Overview

Mock interviews provide realistic practice, valuable feedback, and confidence-building experience before actual interviews.

## Key Areas

### 1. Types of Mock Interviews
- Technical coding interviews
- System design interviews
- Behavioral interviews
- Case study interviews

### 2. Finding Partners
- Peers and colleagues
- Professional coaches
- Online platforms
- Interview prep communities

### 3. Feedback Collection
- Code quality and efficiency
- Communication clarity
- Problem-solving approach
- Time management

### 4. Improvement Strategy
- Analyze feedback patterns
- Focus on weak areas
- Track progress over time
- Adjust preparation approach

## Tips
- Treat it like a real interview
- Record sessions for review
- Practice under time constraints
- Seek diverse interviewers
