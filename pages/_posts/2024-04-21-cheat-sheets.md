## Overview

Cheat sheets provide quick access to essential information, syntax, and commands during interview preparation and revision.

## Key Areas

### 1. Types of Cheat Sheets
- Programming language syntax
- Algorithm complexity
- System design patterns
- Common commands

### 2. Creation Process
- Identify key information
- Organize logically
- Keep concise
- Update regularly

### 3. Usage Strategies
- Quick review sessions
- Before interviews
- During problem-solving
- Revision planning

### 4. Digital vs. Physical
- Digital accessibility
- Physical for better retention
- Portability considerations
- Search functionality

## Tips
- Focus on high-frequency information
- Use visual elements
- Keep updated
- Test yourself regularly
