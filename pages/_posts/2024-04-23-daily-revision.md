## Overview

Daily revision ensures consistent learning, prevents cramming, and builds strong long-term knowledge retention.

## Key Areas

### 1. Daily Routine
- Fixed time slot
- Focused sessions
- Progress tracking
- Reward system

### 2. Content Selection
- Previous day's topics
- Weak areas
- High-frequency concepts
- Mixed topics

### 3. Methods
- Active recall
- Practice problems
- Concept review
- Self-quizzing

### 4. Duration Planning
- Short focused sessions
- Quality over quantity
- Break management
- Energy optimization

## Tips
- Start small and build consistency
- Track your progress
- Adjust based on energy levels
- Make it enjoyable
