## Overview

Effective salary negotiation ensures you're compensated fairly while maintaining a positive relationship with your future employer.

## Key Areas

### 1. Market Research
- Industry salary benchmarks
- Location-based adjustments
- Company size and stage factors
- Role-specific ranges

### 2. Total Compensation
- Base salary
- Equity and stock options
- Bonuses and incentives
- Benefits and perks

### 3. Negotiation Strategy
- Know your worth
- Be prepared to justify
- Consider non-monetary benefits
- Have a clear BATNA

### 4. Communication Tactics
- Professional tone
- Express enthusiasm
- Be specific with numbers
- Listen actively

## Tips
- Research thoroughly before negotiating
- Practice your pitch
- Be ready to walk away
- Get offers in writing
