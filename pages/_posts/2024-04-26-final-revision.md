## Overview

Final revision in the days leading up to interviews focuses on confidence building, quick reviews, and mental preparation.

## Key Areas

### 1. Last-Minute Topics
- High-frequency questions
- Weak areas
- Company-specific prep
- Recent projects

### 2. Confidence Building
- Review past successes
- Positive self-talk
- Visualize success
- Acknowledge preparation

### 3. Stress Management
- Relaxation techniques
- Sleep optimization
- Physical exercise
- Social support

### 4. Logistics Preparation
- Interview details
- Technology setup
- Environment preparation
- Backup plans

## Tips
- Don't try to learn new topics
- Focus on review and confidence
- Get proper rest
- Stay positive and relaxed
