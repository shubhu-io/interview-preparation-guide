## Overview

Director rounds evaluate strategic thinking, organizational leadership, and ability to align technical initiatives with business objectives at scale.

## Key Areas

### 1. Strategic Planning
- Long-term technical vision
- Resource allocation
- Risk management
- Business alignment

### 2. Organizational Leadership
- Building and scaling teams
- Organizational design
- Culture and values
- Change management

### 3. Budget and Finance
- Cost optimization
- ROI analysis
- Vendor negotiations
- Capital planning

### 4. Cross-department Collaboration
- Engineering and product alignment
- Working with finance and legal
- Executive communication
- Board reporting

## Common Questions
- How do you prioritize initiatives across multiple teams?
- Describe a time you had to make a difficult trade-off between cost and quality
- How do you ensure technical decisions support business goals?
- What's your approach to building a culture of engineering excellence?
