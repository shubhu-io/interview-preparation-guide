## Overview

Bar raisers ensure hiring standards are maintained across the organization. They evaluate candidates independently and focus on leadership principles and long-term potential.

## Key Areas

### 1. Leadership Principles
- Customer obsession
- Ownership and accountability
- Bias for action
- Dive deep

### 2. Behavioral Assessment
- Past experiences
- Decision-making process
- Conflict handling
- Growth mindset

### 3. Cross-team Evaluation
- Team fit assessment
- Collaboration potential
- Communication skills
- Cultural contribution

### 4. Hiring Standards
- Quality bar maintenance
- Long-term potential
- Skill development capacity
- Adaptability

## Common Questions
- Describe a time you disagreed with a decision but supported it
- How do you handle failure?
- Tell me about a time you went above and beyond
- How do you ensure quality in your work?
