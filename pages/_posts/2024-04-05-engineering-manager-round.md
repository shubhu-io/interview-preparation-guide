## Overview

The engineering manager round focuses on your ability to lead technical teams, drive engineering excellence, and align technology with business goals.

## Key Areas

### 1. Technical Strategy
- Technology vision and roadmap
- Build vs. buy decisions
- Technical debt management
- Innovation and experimentation

### 2. Team Development
- Hiring and onboarding
- Career development for reports
- Mentorship and coaching
- Performance management

### 3. Process Excellence
- Development workflows
- Code quality standards
- Testing strategies
- Deployment practices

### 4. Cross-functional Collaboration
- Working with product teams
- Stakeholder communication
- Resource planning
- Priority alignment

## Common Questions
- How do you balance technical debt with feature delivery?
- Describe your approach to building a high-performing team
- How do you handle disagreements with product managers?
- What engineering practices have you introduced that improved team productivity?
