## Overview

Presentation skills are essential for senior engineers and technical leaders. This guide covers how to deliver effective technical presentations in interview settings.

## Key Areas

### 1. Structure Your Presentation
- **Opening**: Hook the audience, state your purpose
- **Body**: Logical flow, key points, supporting evidence
- **Conclusion**: Summary, call to action, memorable closing

### 2. Content Organization
- Start with the problem statement
- Explain your approach and reasoning
- Demonstrate technical depth
- Share results and impact
- Discuss lessons learned

### 3. Delivery Tips
- Practice multiple times
- Time yourself
- Prepare for questions
- Use clear, concise language
- Maintain eye contact

### 4. Common Scenarios
- Project walkthrough
- System design presentation
- Technical deep-dive
- Architecture review
- Post-mortem analysis

## Preparation Checklist
- [ ] Create clear slides/visuals
- [ ] Practice timing
- [ ] Prepare for Q&A
- [ ] Review technical details
- [ ] Have backup plans
