## Overview

Joining preparation ensures a smooth transition into your new role, making a positive first impression and setting yourself up for early success.

## Key Areas

### 1. Pre-boarding Tasks
- Complete paperwork
- Set up accounts
- Review company materials
- Plan first day

### 2. First Day Preparation
- Professional attire
- Transportation planning
- Necessary documents
- Early arrival

### 3. Team Introduction
- Learn team names and roles
- Understand team structure
- Identify key stakeholders
- Schedule introductory meetings

### 4. Early Success Strategy
- Set 30-60-90 day goals
- Understand expectations
- Find a mentor
- Start building relationships

## Tips
- Be enthusiastic and positive
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Take detailed notes
- Follow up on introductions
