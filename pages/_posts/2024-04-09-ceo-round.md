## Overview

CEO rounds evaluate alignment with company vision, business understanding, and cultural fit at the highest level.

## Key Areas

### 1. Company Vision Understanding
- Mission and values alignment
- Industry perspective
- Growth potential
- Competitive advantages

### 2. Business Acumen
- Revenue model understanding
- Market dynamics
- Customer needs
- Financial literacy

### 3. Culture Fit
- Leadership philosophy
- Work ethics
- Team building approach
- Decision-making style

### 4. Long-term Commitment
- Career goals alignment
- Growth expectations
- Contribution potential
- Retention factors

## Common Questions
- Why do you want to join our company?
- How do you see technology contributing to our mission?
- What would you do in the first 90 days?
- Describe your leadership philosophy
