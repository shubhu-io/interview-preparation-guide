## Why Aptitude Matters

- **70%+ of companies** use aptitude tests as initial screening
- Tests measure raw cognitive ability, not just learned skills
- Strong scores predict job performance across roles
- Standardized evaluation across all candidates

## Quick Tips

1. **Master fundamentals** before attempting advanced problems
2. **Practice daily** with timed conditions
3. **Learn shortcuts** for common calculation patterns
4. **Review mistakes** to avoid repetition
5. **Stay consistent** - regular practice beats cramming
