# Previous Year Questions for Interview Preparation

Analyzing previous year questions helps identify patterns, common topics, and the actual difficulty level of interviews at various companies.

## Introduction

Previous year questions (PYQs) provide real insights into what companies actually ask. They help you understand difficulty levels, topic distribution, and interview patterns.

## Key Concepts

### Value of PYQs
- Reveals actual interview patterns
- Shows difficulty levels
- Highlights high-frequency topics
- Provides realistic practice
- Reduces surprise on interview day

### Sources of PYQs
- Online platforms (LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks)
- Interview preparation websites
- Company-specific forums
- Professional networks
- Interview coaching resources

### PYQ Analysis Framework
| Aspect | What to Analyze |
|--------|----------------|
| Topics | Which topics appear most? |
| Difficulty | Easy, medium, hard ratio |
| Patterns | Common problem types |
| Company | Company-specific trends |
| Role | Role-specific requirements |

### How to Use PYQs
1. Collect questions from reliable sources
2. Categorize by topic and difficulty
3. Practice solving them
4. Analyze patterns and trends
5. Adjust preparation accordingly

## FAQ

### Q1: Are PYQs reliable?
They provide good patterns, but actual interviews vary. Use them as guidance, not gospel.

### Q2: How many PYQs should I practice?
100-200 questions covering all major topics.

### Q3: Should I memorize PYQ answers?
No. Understand the approach, not just the solution.

### Q4: Where can I find PYQs?
LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks, InterviewBit, and company-specific forums.

### Q5: Are PYQs the same as actual interviews?
No. They provide patterns, but actual questions vary.

### Q6: Should I focus on one company's PYQs?
Balance between company-specific and general preparation.

### Q7: How often do companies repeat questions?
Rarely exact repeats, but patterns and themes repeat often.

### Q8: Should I time myself with PYQs?
Yes. Practice under time pressure to simulate interview conditions.

### Q9: What if I can't solve a PYQ?
Learn from the solution, understand the approach, and revisit later.

### Q10: How do I use PYQs effectively?
Practice, analyze patterns, identify weak areas, and adjust preparation.

## Common Mistakes

1. **Memorizing solutions** - Understand approaches, not just answers
2. **Ignoring patterns** - Focus on trends, not individual questions
3. **Not practicing under pressure** - Time yourself
4. **Only doing easy questions** - Challenge yourself with hard ones
5. **Not analyzing mistakes** - Learn from errors
6. **Using unreliable sources** - Verify question authenticity

## Best Practices

1. Use reliable sources
2. Categorize by topic and difficulty
3. Practice under time pressure
4. Analyze patterns and trends
5. Understand approaches, not just solutions
6. Learn from mistakes
7. Adjust preparation based on analysis
8. Balance company-specific and general PYQs
