# Interview Rounds

A comprehensive guide to navigating every type of interview round you'll encounter in your career.

## Overview

Interview rounds are the structured stages through which employers evaluate candidates. Each round serves a distinct purpose and tests different competencies. Understanding what each round demands helps you prepare strategically and perform confidently.

## Key Takeaways

- **Preparation is everything** — Each round has its own expectations and evaluation criteria
- **Tailor your approach** — Adapt your communication style to the interviewer's level and focus
- **Use structured frameworks** — STAR method, storytelling, and clear articulation win every time
- **Show impact** — Quantify results whenever possible
- **Be authentic** — Interviewers detect rehearsed or insincere answers quickly

## Preparation Strategy

1. **Map the interview process** — Know which rounds to expect and in what order
2. **Research the company** — Culture, products, recent news, and strategic direction
3. **Prepare your stories** — 8-12 strong examples covering leadership, conflict, success, and failure
4. **Practice aloud** — Verbal delivery is different from silent thinking
5. **Prepare questions** — Thoughtful questions demonstrate genuine interest and strategic thinking
