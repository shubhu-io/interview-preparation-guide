## Overview

Offer discussion involves evaluating the complete package, negotiating terms, and making an informed decision about your next career move.

## Key Areas

### 1. Offer Evaluation
- Complete compensation package
- Role and responsibilities
- Growth opportunities
- Company stability

### 2. Negotiation Points
- Salary adjustments
- Equity discussions
- Start date flexibility
- Remote work options

### 3. Contract Review
- Employment terms
- Non-compete clauses
- Intellectual property rights
- Termination policies

### 4. Decision Framework
- Pros and cons analysis
- Long-term career impact
- Personal circumstances
- Gut feeling consideration

## Questions to Ask
- What's the growth path for this role?
- How is performance evaluated?
- What's the equity vesting schedule?
- What benefits are included?
