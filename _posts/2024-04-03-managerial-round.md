## Overview

Managerial rounds assess your leadership capabilities, decision-making skills, and ability to manage teams and projects effectively.

## Key Areas

### 1. Leadership Style
- Situational leadership
- Empowering team members
- Setting clear expectations
- Providing constructive feedback

### 2. Conflict Resolution
- Handling team disagreements
- Mediating between stakeholders
- Managing difficult conversations
- Finding win-win solutions

### 3. Project Management
- Planning and estimation
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Resource allocation
- Delivery accountability

### 4. Decision Making
- Data-driven decisions
- Balancing speed vs. quality
- Handling ambiguity
- Escalation strategies

## Common Questions
- Describe your management style
- How do you handle underperforming team members?
- Tell me about a project that failed and what you learned
- How do you prioritize tasks when everything seems urgent?
