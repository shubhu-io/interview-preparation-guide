## Overview

Monthly revision provides a comprehensive review of your preparation progress and helps adjust strategies for optimal results.

## Key Areas

### 1. Comprehensive Review
- Topic coverage analysis
- Skill development assessment
- Interview performance review
- Resource utilization

### 2. Progress Metrics
- Mock interview scores
- Problem-solving speed
- Knowledge retention
- Confidence levels

### 3. Strategy Adjustment
- Identify what works
- Modify ineffective methods
- Update learning materials
- Refocus priorities

### 4. Long-term Planning
- Set monthly milestones
- Track career goals
- Update preparation plan
- Adjust timelines

## Tips
- Review all notes and materials
- Assess weak areas honestly
- Update your preparation plan
- Set clear goals for next month
