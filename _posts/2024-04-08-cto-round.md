## Overview

CTO rounds assess your technical vision, architecture decision-making, and ability to lead the entire engineering organization's technical direction.

## Key Areas

### 1. Technology Vision
- Industry best practices
- Emerging technologies
- Technology stack decisions
- Platform strategy

### 2. Architecture Decisions
- System design philosophy
- Scalability strategies
- Security considerations
- Performance optimization

### 3. Technical Standards
- Code quality standards
- Documentation practices
- Review processes
- Compliance requirements

### 4. Team Scaling
- Hiring strategy
- Team structure design
- Knowledge sharing
- Career progression

## Common Questions
- How do you evaluate and adopt new technologies?
- Describe your approach to technical due diligence
- How do you ensure engineering quality at scale?
- What's your philosophy on building technical teams?
