## Overview

Weekly revision sessions consolidate learning, assess progress, and plan improvements for the coming week.

## Key Areas

### 1. Session Structure
- Review daily notes
- Practice problems
- Concept consolidation
- Planning next week

### 2. Progress Assessment
- Topic coverage
- Strength identification
- Weakness areas
- Time management

### 3. Focus Areas
- Topics needing more practice
- New concepts introduced
- Interview preparation gaps
- Skill development needs

### 4. Planning
- Set weekly goals
- Allocate study time
- Choose practice methods
- Schedule mock interviews

## Tips
- Keep sessions structured
- Be honest about progress
- Adjust strategies as needed
- Celebrate small wins
