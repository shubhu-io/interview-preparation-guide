## Overview

Culture fit assessment ensures you'll thrive in the company's environment and contribute positively to the team's dynamics.

## Key Areas

### 1. Company Values Research
- Mission statement understanding
- Core values identification
- Cultural norms awareness
- Work environment style

### 2. Self-Assessment
- Work style preferences
- Communication preferences
- Decision-making approach
- Conflict resolution style

### 3. Demonstrating Fit
- Share relevant experiences
- Show value alignment
- Demonstrate adaptability
- Express genuine interest

### 4. Red Flags to Avoid
- Misalignment with core values
- Poor communication fit
- Incompatible work style
- Lack of genuine interest

## Tips
- Research the company thoroughly
- Be authentic in responses
- Share specific examples
- Ask about team culture
