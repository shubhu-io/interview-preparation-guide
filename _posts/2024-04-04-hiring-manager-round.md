## Overview

The hiring manager round evaluates mutual fit between you and the team. It's your chance to understand the role while demonstrating you're the right candidate.

## Key Areas

### 1. Role Understanding
- Day-to-day responsibilities
- Success metrics
- Growth opportunities
- Team dynamics

### 2. Cultural Fit
- Work style alignment
- Values match
- Communication preferences
- Collaboration approach

### 3. Technical Alignment
- Required skills assessment
- Technology stack familiarity
- Learning ability
- Problem-solving approach

### 4. Mutual Evaluation
- Ask thoughtful questions about the team
- Understand challenges they face
- Learn about growth trajectory
- Clarify expectations

## Questions to Ask
- What does success look like in this role?
- What are the biggest challenges the team faces?
- How is performance evaluated?
- What's the team's technical roadmap?

## Tips
- Research the team and manager beforehand
- Be genuine about your interests
- Show enthusiasm for the role
- Discuss long-term career goals
