## Overview

Team fit evaluates how well you'll collaborate with existing team members and contribute to a positive team environment.

## Key Areas

### 1. Collaboration Style
- Working with different personalities
- Supporting team goals
- Sharing knowledge
- Constructive feedback

### 2. Communication Approach
- Active listening
- Clear expression
- Regular updates
- Documentation habits

### 3. Conflict Resolution
- Handling disagreements
- Finding compromises
- Maintaining relationships
- Professional boundaries

### 4. Team Contribution
- Mentoring others
- Knowledge sharing
- Process improvement
- Positive attitude

## Common Questions
- How do you handle working with difficult team members?
- Describe your ideal team environment
- How do you contribute to team morale?
- Tell me about a successful team project you were part of
