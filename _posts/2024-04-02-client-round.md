## Overview

Client rounds focus on your ability to interact with stakeholders, understand business requirements, and communicate technical concepts to non-technical audiences.

## Key Areas

### 1. Understanding Client Needs
- Active listening skills
- Asking clarifying questions
- Identifying pain points
- Translating business needs to technical solutions

### 2. Communication Skills
- Simplify technical jargon
- Use analogies and examples
- Present solutions clearly
- Handle objections professionally

### 3. Technical Presentation
- Demo your solution effectively
- Address concerns proactively
- Show awareness of constraints
- Discuss trade-offs transparently

### 4. Building Rapport
- Show empathy for client challenges
- Demonstrate domain knowledge
- Be honest about limitations
- Follow up on commitments

## Common Questions
- How would you explain [complex concept] to a non-technical stakeholder?
- Tell me about a time you managed client expectations
- How do you handle scope creep?
- Describe your approach to requirements gathering
