## Overview

Company-wise preparation helps you tailor your approach to each company's specific interview process, culture, and requirements.

## Key Areas

### 1. Company Research
- Business model
- Technology stack
- Recent news
- Company culture

### 2. Interview Process
- Round structure
- Evaluation criteria
- Timeline
- Key interviewers

### 3. Role-Specific Prep
- Required skills
- Team structure
- Project types
- Growth opportunities

### 4. Tailored Approach
- Customize examples
- Align with company values
- Prepare relevant questions
- Demonstrate domain interest

## Tips
- Research thoroughly before each interview
- Connect with current employees
- Understand the company's challenges
- Prepare company-specific examples
